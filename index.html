The Photographer
"Nature was my first teacher — light, patience, stillness. Everything I learned chasing it, I now bring to the people in front of my lens."
Celine Potter is a natural light photographer based in Washington D.C. and Maryland, with over 18 years behind the lens. Her work has been published in Seven Magazine and Shoutout Atlanta, shot for Washingtonian Events, and exhibited with Pro Photo DC. She has photographed multiple seasons of Runway 7 at New York Fashion Week. Raised in the hills of rural Kentucky, she's always been drawn to light and the quiet details most people walk past. That eye now moves through people — portraits, couples, families, events, and everything in between. Candid. Intuitive. Rooted in connection.

—Common Questions
Do you travel outside of Washington D.C.?+
Yes. Travel outside the D.C. metro area starts at $50 and is based on distance and logistics. Reach out and we'll work it out.
Can I customize a package?+
Absolutely. The packages above are starting points. If your session needs more time, more locations, or a different scope, send an email and we'll build something that fits.
When will I receive my photos?+
Your edited gallery is delivered within two weeks via a private Google Photos link. Need them sooner? Rush delivery (under one week) is available for $75.
What if I need to reschedule?+
One reschedule is allowed with at least 48 hours notice at no charge. Less than 48 hours notice incurs a $50 rebooking fee. The $100 booking deposit is non-refundable in all cases.
Do you shoot film?+
Yes — film is available as an add-on for select sessions. Because of processing costs and turnaround, it's priced separately. Ask about it when you reach out.
